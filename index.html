The Problem

Managing field teams is broken

Every company with field employees faces the same three problems. Every single day.

01
Tasks assigned. No proof they happened.
You send an employee to a client site. They say it’s done. You have no photo, no signature, no GPS confirmation. You find out three days later it wasn’t.
02
Attendance sheets are manual, late, and wrong.
Paper registers, WhatsApp photos, Excel files — none of it is real-time. By the time you process attendance, payroll has already been a guess.
03
Field employees unsupervised for hours.
Once they leave the office, you’re blind. Are they at the client? On route? At home? Without a live view, you can’t know — and you can’t prove anything.
What TaskRoom does

Everything you need.
Nothing you don’t.

📍
Live GPS + Full Route Trace
See every employee’s exact location in real-time. Review the complete route they took throughout the day — second by second.
✅
Multi-Step Tasks with Proof
Break tasks into steps. Each step can require a photo, customer signature, or GPS confirmation before the employee can proceed.
🕐
Attendance Sessions
Automatic punch-in/out tied to task activity. Multiple sessions per day. Full history. Export to Excel or PDF instantly.
🔔
Push Notifications
Employees get notified the moment a task is assigned. Managers get alerts when tasks are overdue or steps are missed.
📊
Productivity Scores
Weekly scores for every employee combining task completion rate and hours worked. See your top performers at a glance.
🏢
Room-Based Team Structure
Organise teams into rooms — by city, department, or project. Managers see only their rooms. Total multi-tenant isolation.
Our differentiator
The only tool that shows proof — not just presence.

GPS alone is table-stakes. Every competitor has it. What TaskRoom has is GPS + photo + customer signature + geo-fenced step completion — all in a single task flow. That’s genuinely hard to build, and genuinely valuable to any manager who’s been burnt by employees faking check-ins.

Field Employee Tracking Software

TaskRoom is field employee tracking software that helps managers track the real-time GPS location of field staff, assign multi-step tasks with proof of work, manage attendance, and monitor employee productivity from a single dashboard.

GPS Workforce Management App

With live GPS tracking, geofencing, photo proof collection, digital signature capture, and route history — TaskRoom is the complete GPS workforce management app for delivery teams, field sales, construction, pharma, logistics, and facility management.

Field Force Management Software

TaskRoom field force management software lets you create task rooms, assign tasks with step-by-step workflows, require photo and signature proof at each step, and track employee location in real time.

Employee Attendance Management System

Automated punch-in and punch-out attendance tracking with multiple sessions per day, full attendance history, and one-click PDF and Excel export for payroll processing.

Delivery Workforce Management

Track delivery boys and field agents in real time. Assign delivery tasks, monitor route history, collect photo proof of delivery, and get customer signature digitally on the employee's phone.

Field Sales Tracking Software

Track pharma field reps, sales executives, and on-ground agents. Verify client visits with GPS geo-fencing, photo proof, and customer signature. Stop fake check-ins with tamper-proof audit trails.

Construction Workforce Management

Manage site supervisors and field workers across multiple job sites. Assign tasks, track location, collect progress photos, and monitor attendance across all construction sites from one dashboard.

Task Management Software for Field Teams

Create multi-step task workflows for field employees. Each step can require GPS verification, photo upload, or customer signature before the employee can proceed. Get real-time task progress updates.
